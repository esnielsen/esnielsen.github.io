Below is my entry for the 2020 ‘Dance Your PhD’ video contest created by Gonzo Labs and Science Magazine.

With this video I aimed to describe a chapter of my PhD via dance, but also hoped to achieve the following: - make concepts of population genetics accessible to those with little previous knowledge on the subject - engage and collaborate with local dance groups to conduct knowledge transfer between the sciences and the arts - create a unique video that combines my love of dance and science, and have fun while doing it!

This project is dedicated to my family, whom I hope understands what my PhD is about after watching this video :)
